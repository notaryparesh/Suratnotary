About Us

Advocate & Notary Paresh Joshi

With over 15 years of experience in legal practice, Advocate Paresh Joshi has established himself as a trusted name in Surat's legal community. As a qualified Notary Public appointed by the Government, he provides authentic notarization services that hold legal validity across India.

His expertise spans across various domains of law, including criminal defense, civil litigation, property law, and family law.
